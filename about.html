    • OUR STORY

    A cozy coffee shop offers various coffee beans and coffee-related products such as coffee machines, workshops, and activities. Each month, the shop provides special coffee beans imported from around the world, which customers can pre-order before they arrive. Also have a membership program with benefits like early pre-orders for special beans (3 days in advance), exclusive prices on select workshops and activities like the Coffee Lab, where customers can taste various beans paired with different drinks, a 10% discount on drinks (excluding those made with special beans), and more. It’s the perfect spot for anyone looking to explore different types of coffee or for true coffee lovers.

    Carefully Selected Beans

    At 92C, everything starts with the bean. We carefully select high-quality, organic coffee beans from some of the best sources around the world. Each origin is chosen not just for its quality, but for its unique character and flavor. Our goal is to bring out the best in every cup—starting from the source.

    Roasted In-House

    We take pride in roasting our beans in-house. This gives us full control over the roasting process and allows us to highlight the distinct notes and aromas of each bean. Whether it’s a light, fruity roast or a deep, bold one, every batch is roasted with precision and passion.

    Brewed for Everyone

    At the heart of 92C is a simple goal: to make a really good cup of coffee for everyone. Whether you're a longtime coffee lover or just starting to explore, our cozy and welcoming space is made for you. We believe good coffee should be approachable, enjoyable, and something you look forward to—every single time.
